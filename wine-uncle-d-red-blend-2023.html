2023 Uncle D's Red Blend

Rich, Smooth, and Approachable

Wine Details

Vintage: 2023

Blend: 40% Cabernet Sauvignon, 30% Merlot, 20% Syrah, 10% Petite Sirah

Alcohol: 13.8%

pH: 3.62

TA: 6.4 g/L

Residual Sugar: 0.6 g/L (Dry)

Production: 710 cases

Bottle Price: $34

Winemaking

Harvest Date: October 10-20, 2023

Barrel Aging: 13 months

Oak: 25% new French & American oak, 75% neutral

Toast Level: Light-Medium

Fermentation: Separately fermented, blended before aging

Bottling Date: November 12, 2024

Tasting Notes

Appearance Brilliant ruby-garnet with purple highlights. Medium to full body with elegant legs reflecting balanced structure and refined character.
Nose Elegant aromatics of red and black cherry, blackberry, and plum. Notes of violet, dried herbs, and subtle oak spice. Hints of black pepper, cocoa, and cedar with earthy undertones and graphite minerality.
Palate Well-structured with fine-grained tannins and vibrant acidity. Flavors of cherry, blackberry, and plum are complemented by notes of black pepper, dark chocolate, and subtle oak. Mid-palate reveals layers of herbs, graphite, and spice. The cooler vintage produces exceptional balance and elegance.
Finish Clean and refined with lingering fruit and spice. The higher percentage of Syrah in this vintage's blend adds peppery complexity and savory notes. This vintage exemplifies the elegance and food-friendliness achievable in cooler years.

Food Pairing

This versatile red blend pairs beautifully with:

Grilled & BBQ Grilled steak, BBQ ribs, burgers, lamb chops, or smoked brisket
Italian Favorites Pasta Bolognese, lasagna, osso buco, or margherita pizza
Comfort Food Pot roast, meatloaf, shepherd's pie, or aged cheddar

Vintage Notes

The 2023 vintage was characterized by cooler temperatures and well-timed rainfall. The extended growing season allowed for gradual flavor development while preserving natural acidity. The higher percentage of Syrah in this vintage's blend was chosen to emphasize the peppery, savory character that pairs so well with food. This vintage showcases Uncle D's Red Blend in a more elegant, refined style with excellent aging potential.

Drinking Window

Peak: 2026-2030
Approachable now but the structure suggests it will develop beautifully through 2032.
