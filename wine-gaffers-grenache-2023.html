2023 Gaffer's Grenache

Bright, Fruit-Forward, and Silky

Wine Details

Vintage: 2023

Alcohol: 13.8%

pH: 3.62

TA: 6.2 g/L

Residual Sugar: 0.4 g/L (Dry)

Production: 310 cases

Bottle Price: $45

Winemaking

Harvest Date: October 15, 2023

Barrel Aging: 13 months

Oak: 20% new French oak, 80% neutral

Toast Level: Light

Fermentation: Native yeast, open-top fermenters

Bottling Date: November 18, 2024

Tasting Notes

Appearance Luminous ruby with pink-orange rim. Medium body with graceful legs reflecting the wine's balanced alcohol and elegance.
Nose Delicate and perfumed with fresh red cherry, wild strawberry, and red currant. Vibrant floral notes of violet and hibiscus intermingle with hints of white pepper, dried thyme, and pink peppercorn. A whisper of wet stone minerality adds complexity.
Palate Exceptionally refined with silky, fine-grained tannins. Bright acidity lifts flavors of cranberry, raspberry, and tart cherry. Mid-palate shows subtle spice, dried herbs, and a mineral edge. The cooler vintage produces a wine of remarkable purity and precision.
Finish Crisp and refreshing with persistent red fruit and herbal notes. Delicate spice and floral nuances linger. This vintage exemplifies Grenache's ability to showcase elegance, finesse, and terroir expression in cooler years.

Food Pairing

This elegant Grenache pairs beautifully with:

Mediterranean Cuisine Herb-crusted lamb, ratatouille, grilled vegetable platters, or Provençal chicken
Charcuterie & Cheese Prosciutto, salami, aged Manchego, or soft-ripened cheeses
Light & Fresh Duck breast with cherry reduction, mushroom tart, or tuna niçoise salad

Vintage Notes

2023 was a cooler vintage with well-timed rainfall and moderate temperatures. The extended growing season allowed for gradual ripening and exceptional flavor development. Later harvest captured ideal phenolic maturity while maintaining vibrant acidity and lower alcohol. This vintage showcases the hillside site's ability to produce Grenache of remarkable elegance and age-worthiness.

Drinking Window

Peak: 2026-2031
Approachable now but will reward patience, aging gracefully through 2033.
