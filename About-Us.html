About BibiVini

BibiVini enhances how California wineries connect with their guests. We complement your existing tasting room experience with beautiful, mobile-optimized digital wine information accessible via QR code—helping wineries turn every visit into a lasting relationship.

The Opportunity

California's wine industry is experiencing significant shifts in consumer behavior. Tasting room traffic has declined substantially in recent years, making every visitor increasingly valuable. Yet guests often leave printed wine sheets behind—along with any opportunity for follow-up or lasting connection.

Consumers still love wine, but they expect experiences that integrate seamlessly with their digital lives.

Our Solution

BibiVini gives wineries a powerful digital tool to complement their existing tasting room materials, helping guests take the experience home in their pocket while maintaining that crucial post-visit connection.

What BibiVini Adds to Your Tasting Room

BibiVini's platform works alongside your existing tasting room materials. Place QR codes on your bar, and guests can instantly access enhanced digital wine information on their phones.

For Your Guests

Interactive tasting notes and vineyard stories, stunning drone footage of the property, food pairing suggestions and serving tips, and shareable content for social media.

For Your Winery

A powerful digital layer without changing your existing workflow. Continue providing the personal, expert-led experience your guests love—while capturing valuable engagement data.

Lasting Connections

Direct purchasing and wine club enrollment options, plus the ability to maintain connections long after the tasting ends.

Why BibiVini Works

Built for California Wineries

We understand that most wineries are small, family-owned operations without dedicated IT staff or massive marketing budgets. BibiVini is affordable, easy to implement, and requires minimal ongoing maintenance.

Designed by Wine Professionals

Founded by Pete, a Certified Sommelier with a decade in California wine country and a degree in Marketing and Psychology from Eastern Michigan University. BibiVini combines deep wine expertise with consumer behavior insights and marketing strategy.

Mobile-First Enhancement

While wineries adapted during the pandemic with virtual tastings and e-commerce upgrades, the tasting room experience stayed largely the same. BibiVini brings that digital sophistication to the bar as an easy add-on—no replacement of your current materials or processes required.

Our Mission

BibiVini exists to help California wineries thrive in the new era of wine tourism. We believe small wineries deserve enterprise-level tools at small-business prices. We believe technology should enhance the human experience, not replace it. And we believe every winery's story deserves to travel home with every guest.

The wine industry is adapting to fundamental shifts in consumer behavior. BibiVini makes that adaptation smoother, more effective, and more sustainable—one QR code, one tasting room, and one connection at a time.
